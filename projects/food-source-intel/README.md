# Local Food Source Intelligence — NYC MVP

## What Is This?
A curated, regularly-updated intelligence guide for chefs, restaurant owners, and serious home cooks in NYC who want to source better ingredients at better prices — but don't have time to research every market, distributor, and seasonal window.

## The Problem
- NYC's food sourcing landscape is fragmented: wholesale markets, specialty distributors, ethnic grocers, farmers markets, direct-from-farm, online platforms
- Knowledge is tribal — passed chef-to-chef, not documented anywhere comprehensive
- Existing resources are either outdated directories or generic listicles
- No one aggregates: what's in season + where to get it + wholesale vs retail pricing + quality tiers

## The Product (MVP)
**"NYC Source Guide" — a paid weekly/monthly intelligence report**

### Content per issue:
1. **What's In Season This Week** — produce, seafood, with peak dates
2. **Source Spotlight** — deep dive on one supplier/market (hours, minimums, delivery, quality notes)
3. **Price Watch** — wholesale vs retail price comparisons for 10-15 key ingredients
4. **New Finds** — recently opened suppliers, new product lines, pop-up markets
5. **Seasonal Playbook** — what to buy now, what to lock in for next month

### Format Options:
- Email newsletter (Buttondown or Beehiiv — free tier to start)
- PDF download (premium, with maps + contact info)
- Eventually: searchable web database

## Target Customers
1. **Independent restaurant owners/chefs** in NYC (primary)
2. **Caterers & meal prep businesses** (secondary)
3. **Serious home cooks / food enthusiasts** (tertiary — lower price tier)

## Pricing
- Free tier: Monthly summary (lead gen)
- **$12/mo** or **$99/yr**: Weekly intelligence + full supplier database
