# NYC Seasonal Sourcing Calendar

*Tri-state region (NY, NJ, CT) + common imports. Peak = best quality & price.*

|------|------------|-------------|-------|
| Ramps | Mid-Mar → Apr | Union Square Greenmarket, Hudson Valley farms | First of the season; prices drop in April |
| Spinach | Mar → Jun | GrowNYC markets, Fadaro | Overwintered = sweeter |
| Kale & chard | Mar → Nov | Greenmarkets | Hardy greens start strong |

| Parsnips | Through Mar | Greenmarkets | Frost-sweetened, excellent now |

### Seafood — Peak
| Item | Peak Window | Best Source | Notes |
|------|------------|-------------|-------|
| Cod (Atlantic) | Jan → Apr | Fulton Fish Market, F. Rozzo | Winter cod is fattiest |
| Oysters (East Coast) | Sep → Apr | Element Seafood, Fulton | R-month rule; Long Island, CT beds |

### What to Lock In Now
- **Ramps**: Pre-order from Greenmarket vendors; they sell out fast
- **Soft-shell crab season** starts mid-April — establish supplier relationship now
- **Spring lamb**: NJ/PA farms taking pre-orders for April delivery

## Key Price Patterns
- **Glut pricing**: July–August produce (tomatoes, corn, zucchini) — buy in bulk, preserve
- **Premium windows**: Ramps (Mar), fiddleheads (Apr), sour cherries (Jun), soft-shell crab (first flush Apr)
- **Off-peak savings**: Oysters are cheapest in Oct–Nov (start of season, high supply)
- **Import vs local**: Hunts Point handles 60% of NYC's produce — imported items cheapest when local alternatives aren't in season
