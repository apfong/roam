🥬 NYC Source Intel — Week of Mar 12

🌿 Ramps are here. First sightings at Union Square Greenmarket. Small quantities, $18-22/lb retail. Pre-order with your vendor now — peak is 3-4 weeks out. Wholesale: $12-15/lb.
🦀 Soft-shell crab season starts mid-April. Call Element Seafood or your Fulton contact now to get on the first-flush list. First arrivals are premium-priced but pristine.
🐟 Winter cod at its best. Atlantic cod from Georges Bank is at peak fat content right now. Whole fish $4-6/lb wholesale at Fulton. Perfect for: brandade, salt cod projects, crudo.
