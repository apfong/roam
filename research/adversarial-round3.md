- Legal issues: Terms of service violations on every major municipality

### 3. Path to $1K MRR in 60 days

### 5. AI Necessity
**Weekend project.** This is just web scraping + basic ETL. The hard part isn't AI—it's legal access and data quality.

### 6. Score: 2/10
Only gets 2 points because the market need is real. Everything else is delusional.
