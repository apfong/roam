## Product Ideas

### 1. **AgentMesh** — Multi-Agent Orchestration Platform
**One-liner:** "Slack for AI agents" — orchestrate multiple AI agents across different platforms with visual workflows

**Problem:** Companies deploy agents on different platforms (OpenClaw, AutoGPT, etc.) but can't coordinate them. Each agent operates in isolation, leading to duplicate work and missed handoffs.

**Target Customer:** AI-forward SMBs, dev agencies, enterprise AI teams (100-1000 employees)

**Revenue Model:** $99/mo per org + $19/agent/month. Recurring SaaS.

**Why Now:** Agent proliferation happening but coordination layer missing. Enterprise adoption hitting 40% integration by end of 2026.

**MVP Difficulty:** 14 days (Discord/Slack-style interface + webhook orchestration)

**Moat Potential:** High (network effects, agent compatibility data, workflow templates)
