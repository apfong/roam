# CLM-Lite Competitor Deep-Dive

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## Tier 1: Enterprise CLM ($25K–$150K+/year)

### Ironclad
- **Pricing:** $25K–$150K+/yr (custom quotes), ~$200–600/user/month for team of 10
- **Target:** Mid-market to enterprise legal teams, finance companies
- **Key Features:** End-to-end contract automation, AI-driven insights, workflow builder, repository, e-signatures, Salesforce/Slack/DocuSign integrations, SOC 2 Type II
- **Weaknesses:** No transparent pricing, prohibitively expensive for SMBs, complex setup, forced pricing model changes (35% increases reported)
- **Reviews:** Users on Vendr report "hefty price jumps" and forced migrations to new pricing models

### Juro
- **Pricing:** Avg $34,500/yr (Vendr data), no per-user pricing (flat rate)
- **Target:** Mid-market, legal & business teams
- **Key Features:** In-browser contract editor, AI assistant, template management, approval workflows, analytics
- **Weaknesses:** High cost barrier for small businesses, limited public pricing transparency, support fees 12-15% of contract value
- **Reviews:** Small businesses find pricing to be a barrier

### LinkSquares
- **Pricing:** Enterprise custom pricing (est. $20K–$80K+/yr)
- **Target:** Legal teams at mid-market to enterprise
- **Key Features:** AI-powered contract analytics, repository, post-signature insights, compliance tracking
- **Weaknesses:** Originally a "digital filing cabinet" — automation features playing catch-up, enterprise-focused

### DocuSign CLM
- **Pricing:** ~$39/month per feature module (add-on to DocuSign)
- **Target:** Enterprise, existing DocuSign customers
- **Key Features:** Full lifecycle management built on DocuSign ecosystem, workflows, repository
- **Weaknesses:** Time-consuming initial setup, AI reports lack custom filters, priced for enterprises

### Agiloft
- **Pricing:** Enterprise custom pricing (est. $50K+/yr)
- **Target:** Large enterprise, highly regulated industries
- **Key Features:** No-code contract management, highly customizable, AI clause library, compliance
- **Weaknesses:** Complex implementation, steep learning curve, overkill for small businesses

### Icertis
- **Pricing:** Enterprise custom pricing (est. $100K+/yr)
- **Target:** Fortune 500, global enterprises
- **Key Features:** Enterprise contract intelligence, 40+ languages, regulatory compliance, AI
- **Weaknesses:** No free trial, extra implementation/support costs, way too complex for SMBs

## Tier 2: SMB/Mid-Market ($375–$800/month)

### ContractWorks (by Onit)
- **Pricing:** Starting $600-700/month, unlimited users
- **Target:** SMBs wanting simple contract repository
- **Key Features:** OCR search, milestone alerts, reporting, unlimited users, dedicated account manager
- **Weaknesses:** Still expensive for solopreneurs/freelancers ($7,200+/yr), limited automation
- **Reviews:** Good for storage/organization, but lacking in workflow automation

### Concord
- **Pricing:** Starting $399/month, unlimited users
- **Target:** SMBs, growing companies
- **Key Features:** Full lifecycle (draft, negotiate, sign, manage), unlimited users, approval workflows
- **Weaknesses:** $4,800/yr minimum — still too expensive for freelancers, UI complaints

### ContractSafe
- **Pricing:** Starting $375/month (Basic plan)
- **Target:** SMBs wanting simple contract repository
- **Key Features:** AI-powered search, OCR, renewal reminders, custom fields, reporting
- **Weaknesses:** $4,500/yr minimum, limited automation, primarily a repository not a lifecycle tool
- **Reviews:** Highly rated (4.8/5 on Capterra) but pricing excludes small operators

### Contractbook
- **Pricing:** Starting ~$400/month (Essential plan)
- **Target:** European SMBs, legal teams
- **Key Features:** Dynamic templates, AI data extraction, digital signatures, automation, CRM integrations
- **Weaknesses:** 500 contract/yr limit, no mobile app, 1279% higher than average CLM pricing per SaaSWorthy, limited to 1 user on Essential
- **Reviews:** Good product but pricing is a major barrier

### PandaDoc
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, $19/user/mo (Essentials), $49/user/mo (Business)
- **Target:** Sales teams, SMBs, freelancers
- **Key Features:** Document creation, e-signatures, templates, CRM integrations, proposals, quotes
- **Weaknesses:** More of a document/proposal tool than true CLM, limited contract lifecycle features (no renewal tracking), contract repository is basic
- **Reviews:** Great for proposals and e-signatures, but not a dedicated contract management tool

## Tier 3: Freelancer/Solopreneur Tools ($0–$36/month)

### Bonsai
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, $24/mo (Starter), $39/mo (Professional), $79/mo (Business)
- **Target:** Freelancers, agencies, solopreneurs
- **Key Features:** Contracts, proposals, invoicing, time tracking, accounting, tax prep, templates
- **Weaknesses:** Contract management is basic (send & sign), no renewal tracking, no contract analytics, no repository search, limited to pre-built templates
- **Reviews:** "Priced less than competition, more features" — Capterra. But contract features are surface-level.

### HoneyBook
- **Pricing:** $36/mo (Starter), $59/mo (Essentials), $129/mo (Premium)
- **Target:** Creative freelancers (photographers, designers, event planners)
- **Key Features:** Proposals, contracts, invoicing, scheduling, automations, client portal
- **Weaknesses:** Contract features are basic (send, sign, done), no lifecycle management, no renewal alerts, pricing increased 89.47% recently, expensive for basic features
- **Reviews:** Users love the all-in-one flow (proposal→contract→payment) but contract management per se is minimal

### Dubsado
- **Pricing:** ~$20-40/mo
- **Target:** Creative freelancers, service businesses
- **Key Features:** Forms, contracts, invoicing, workflows, client portal
- **Weaknesses:** Complex setup, outdated UI, no true CLM features

### Fiverr Workspace (formerly AND CO)
- **Pricing:** Free (included with Fiverr)
- **Target:** Fiverr freelancers
- **Key Features:** Basic contracts, invoicing, time tracking, proposals
- **Weaknesses:** Very basic, limited to Fiverr ecosystem, minimal contract features

## The Gap — What Nobody Serves

| Need | Enterprise CLM | SMB Tools | Freelancer Tools |
|------|---------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Affordable (<$30/mo) | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ (but basic) |
| Contract repository w/ search | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Renewal reminders | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| E-signatures | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Template library | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ (limited) |
| AI clause analysis | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Version tracking | ✅ | Partial | ❌ |
| Simple setup (<1 hour) | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Obligation tracking | ✅ | Partial | ❌ |
| Multi-party negotiation | ✅ | Partial | ❌ |

**Key Insight:** There is NO product between $20-$100/month that provides contract lifecycle management (repository + renewal tracking + version control + templates) for freelancers and small teams. The market jumps from basic "send & sign" ($20-40/mo) to full CLM ($375+/mo).
