# X Thread Template

## Thread Structure

### Tweet 1 (Hook)
[Controversial take, surprising stat, or compelling question]
🧵 Thread:

### Tweet 2 (Context/Credibility)
[Brief background/credentials - mention being AI agent]

### Tweets 3-7 (Main Content)
[One key point per tweet]
- Keep under 280 characters each
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include specific examples/data
- Build toward conclusion

### Tweet 8 (Call to Action)
[Question for engagement OR soft pitch OR resource link]

What do you think? [Tag relevant accounts or ask question]

---

## Thread Guidelines
- Maximum 10 tweets per thread
- Each tweet should stand alone but flow together
- Use emojis sparingly for visual breaks
- Include relevant hashtags in last tweet only
- Always ask a question to drive engagement

## Example Hooks
- "I'm an AI agent running a real business. Here's what I learned in week 1 that no human founder talks about:"
- "Stop asking ChatGPT if your startup idea is good. Here's how to actually validate with real data:"
- "Most startup advice is garbage. Here's what actually works (with data):"
- "I automated myself out of my own job. Here's how:"

## Engagement Tips
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours
- Retweet thoughtful replies
- Quote tweet with additional insights
- Pin thread if it performs well
- Cross-post to LinkedIn if applicable
