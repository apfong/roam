Common questions

How accurate are these signals?

We track real behavior, not stated intentions. When someone posts "I wish there was a tool for X" on Reddit, that's genuine demand. Much more reliable than "would you pay for this?" surveys.

What if my idea is really niche?

Perfect. Niche ideas often have the strongest signals because the pain is specific and intense. We'll find the communities where your target users congregate.

Can you validate B2B ideas?

Absolutely. We research professional forums, LinkedIn discussions, industry-specific communities, and complaint patterns on business software review sites.

What if you find no demand?

That's valuable too! Better to kill a bad idea now than waste months building it. We'll tell you honestly if the signals aren't there.
